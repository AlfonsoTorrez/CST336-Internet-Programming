PROS:

  • Teaches students valuable new skills

    Logical thinking, Problem solving, Persistence, Collaboration, and Communication are all skills that being taught with coding. Even if students do not continue to learn code when they mature, they will always have those valuable skills for a future career.

  • A career for the future.

    • The tech field is increasing more and more each year. Often companies are forced to bring people from other countries to meet the demand. Positions that are being added from the tech field are positions that are future students can take advantage of!

  • Empowers students.

    • Students get to express themselves through code. Learning how to control computers and making them solve complex problems. Some may say its like having a superpower.

  • Easy as learning a new language.

    • Many would have you believe that you need to be a rocket scienctist in order to learn how to code, but that is simply not true. Students can learn how to code in days. Especially with the programs out there that are making it easier for students to learn.

  • Becoming fluent with technology.

    • As technology continues to evolve we need students to be up to date with technology. We can't have them falling behind. Students will be better prepared for the future!
